Sri Lankan baked chicken

Ingredients

8 chicken thighs, skinned
½ onion, quartered
5 cloves garlic
1.5cm piece ginger
1 tbsp chilli powder
½ tsp ground coriande
1 tsp ground turmeric
5 black peppercorns
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp caster sugar
90ml vegetable oil
4 tbsp tomato ketchup
½ lime, juiced
1 tsp sea salt
2 bay leaves

Method

STEP 1
Put all the marinade ingredients into a food processor and whizz until you
have a coarse paste. Put the chicken into a shallow ovenproof dish and
pour over the marinade. Mix well, cover and marinate for 30 minutes.
STEP 2
Heat the oven to 180C/fan 160C/gas 4. Cover the dish with foil, poke
several large holes in it and bake for 60 minutes until the chicken is
cooked and the sauce has reduced.
